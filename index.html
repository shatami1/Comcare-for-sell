Agent Responsibilities

What agents handle for each order

✅

Pick up from warehouse

Collect equipment from the warehouse before each delivery.

✅

Deliver to customer

Deliver equipment, confirm placement, and provide basic handoff guidance.

✅

Pickup after rental

Schedule pickup with the customer and retrieve the equipment when the rental ends.

✅

Return to warehouse

Bring returned items back to the warehouse after pickup.
